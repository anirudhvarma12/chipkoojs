chipkoojs

Chipkoo (meaning sticky) is a jquery plugin to fix table headers. Chipkoo does not need to know the total width of your table. Once you attach it to a table, it automatically calculates everything that is required & makes the header sticky.

$(".js-fixHead").chipkoo();
